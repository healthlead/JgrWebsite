About JGR Construction

Building Miami's future with excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to quality since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2009, JGR Construction emerged from a vision to transform Miami's skyline through superior structural contracting. Our founders, seasoned professionals with decades of combined experience, recognized the need for a construction company that could deliver both innovative solutions and unwavering quality.

What started as a small team of dedicated craftsmen has evolved into one of South Florida's most trusted structural contracting companies. We've built our reputation project by project, always putting quality, safety, and client satisfaction at the forefront of everything we do.

Today, JGR Construction stands as a testament to what's possible when expertise meets dedication. We continue to push the boundaries of what's achievable in structural concrete and masonry construction.
