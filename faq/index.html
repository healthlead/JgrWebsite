Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, processes, and expertise

General Services

JGR Construction specializes in structural concrete and masonry construction for both residential and commercial projects. Our expertise includes conventional concrete shells, post-tension concrete, precast concrete, masonry construction, architectural concrete, and emergency repairs.

Yes, we handle both residential and commercial projects. From high-rise residential towers and luxury condominiums to commercial office buildings, retail spaces, and mixed-use developments, we have the expertise to deliver quality results across all project types.

We primarily serve Miami-Dade County and surrounding areas in South Florida. Our headquarters is located in Medley, FL, and we work throughout the greater Miami metropolitan area including Brickell, Wynwood, Coral Gables, Aventura, and the Design District.

Licensing & Safety

Yes, JGR Construction is fully licensed with the State of Florida (CGC License #1234567) and carries comprehensive general liability and workers' compensation insurance. We are also bonded to ensure complete protection for our clients.

Safety is our top priority. We maintain OSHA compliance, conduct regular safety training for all team members, implement comprehensive safety protocols, and have achieved recognition through the OSHA Voluntary Protection Program. Our safety director oversees all projects to ensure the highest safety standards.

All our workers are properly trained and certified in their respective specialties. We maintain ongoing training programs, ensure OSHA certification for all team members, and regularly update our training protocols to reflect the latest industry standards and best practices.

Project Management

Project timelines vary depending on size, complexity, and scope. A typical high-rise residential project may take 12-18 months, while smaller commercial projects can be completed in 3-6 months. We provide detailed project schedules during the planning phase and maintain a 98% on-time completion rate.

Yes, we provide comprehensive project management services including planning, scheduling, quality control, safety oversight, and coordination with other trades. Our experienced project managers ensure smooth execution from start to finish.

We understand that project requirements can evolve. We maintain open communication with clients and provide detailed change order processes. All modifications are documented, priced transparently, and approved before implementation to avoid surprises.

Quality & Materials

We implement comprehensive quality control at every stage, including material inspection upon delivery, regular progress inspections, adherence to engineering specifications, final quality audits, and detailed documentation. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified for quality management.

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. We provide structural warranties as required by Florida law and additional warranties on our workmanship. Specific warranty terms are outlined in each project contract.

We use only high-quality materials from trusted suppliers. This includes various concrete mixes designed for specific applications, premium reinforcing steel, high-quality masonry units, and specialized additives. All materials meet or exceed industry standards and project specifications.

Emergency Services

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency repair services for structural concrete and masonry issues. Our emergency response team can quickly assess damage, implement immediate safety measures, and provide permanent repair solutions to ensure building safety and integrity.

We maintain a 24/7 emergency response capability and typically respond to emergency calls within 2-4 hours. For critical structural issues, we can mobilize our emergency team immediately to assess the situation and implement safety measures.
